Tokio Hotel est un groupe de pop rock allemand, originaire de Magdebourg. Formé en 2001, et constitué de quatre jeunes Allemands, dont des frères jumeaux Tom et Bill Kaulitz, le groupe compte au total sept albums : Devilish (2003), Schrei (2005), Zimmer 483 / Scream (2007), Humanoid (2009), Kings of Suburbia (2014), Dream Machine (2017) et 2001 (2022).

Living on the tropical island of Grenada in the Caribbean, marc indigo creates music that fuses emotional indie vibes with energetic elements of pop, hip hop and r&b to pioneer his own genre of ‘hyperindie’. Marc has been releasing music for years but now has hit his stride in creating the music he was meant to create. With global support from various publications, YouTube channels and an international fan base, Marc is just getting started.

RJ pasin musical artist. He rose to popularity on TikTok at rjpasin, sharing his original compositions and productions.
